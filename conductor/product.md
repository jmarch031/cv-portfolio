Un portfolio design style awaaards (site of the day, site of the year) mettant en avant ma philosophie, mes skills, mes expériences, mes formations, mes articles medium et projets

# Product Guide

## Vision
To create a high-fidelity, interactive professional portfolio for Julien Marchand that stands among the best-designed sites on the web (Awwwards "Site of the Day" level). The platform will blend technical excellence with strategic leadership vision, reflecting a "Balanced & Strategic" tone of voice.

## Target Audience
- **Recruiters & Engineering Leaders**: Seeking top-tier Engineering Management and technical talent.
- **Tech Community**: Peers and potential collaborators interested in software architecture and leadership.
- **Potential Clients**: Looking for strategic technical advisory or high-quality development.

## Key Features & Focus Areas
- **Management Case Studies**: Detailed narratives of leadership journeys, including supply chain modernization and team restructuring.
- **Recent Technical Projects**: Showcasing modern development skills with a focus on code quality and performance.
- **Thought Leadership**: Integrated section for Medium articles and personal reflections on Engineering Management and Quality Engineering.
- **Primary Conversion**: A prominent "Démarrer une conversation" call-to-action leading directly to email contact.

## Interactive Experience (Awwwards-Level)
- **Dynamic Filtering**: Seamless content sorting for projects and articles without page reloads.
- **Data Visualizations**: Interactive representations of technical skills, project timelines, or DORA metrics.
- **Enhanced Micro-interactions**: Unique transitions, custom cursor behaviors, and GSAP-driven scroll effects that provide a premium, tactile feel.
- **Dual-Language Support**: Full French and English localization (i18n) to reach a global audience.
