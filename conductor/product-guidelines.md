# Product Guidelines

## Content & Tone
- **Narrative Voice**: The writing should be "Accessible & Narrative," telling the story of leadership and impact rather than just listing technical specs.
- **Balanced Hierarchy**: Content follows a "Balanced & Holistic" approach, giving equal weight to technical architecture and strategic leadership achievements.
- **Language**: Bilingual (FR/EN) with a consistent, punchy tone in both languages.

## Visual & Branding
- **"Awwwards" Excellence**: High-fidelity design with cinematic storytelling. Maximize the use of GSAP, scroll-driven reveals, and smooth, immersive transitions.
- **Design Language**: Glassmorphism with deep, saturated accent colors (indigo, orange, green) and high-quality typography.
- **Tactile Feedback**: Every interaction must feel premium and "tactile," providing subtle, non-obstructive visual feedback on every action.

## UX & Accessibility
- **Motion Accessibility**: Provide a dedicated "Reduced Motion Control" for users who prefer a more static experience.
- **Reading Focus**: Implement a "Focus Mode" for long-form content (like Medium articles) to simplify the layout and reduce distractions during reading.
- **Non-Obstructive Interactivity**: Ensure that custom cursor and background "glow" effects do not obscure critical information or clickable elements.
- **Performance First**: Despite high interactivity, the site must maintain a smooth frame rate (targeted 60fps) using hardware-accelerated CSS and optimized GSAP timelines.
